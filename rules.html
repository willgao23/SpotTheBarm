How to Play

Welcome to Spot the Barm! The rules are pretty simple: find Barm and don't let your time run out.
Once you start a new game, you'll see a timer start counting down. That's how much time you have to find Barm. Every time you 'spot' Barm by clicking on him, you'll get five more seconds and one point added to your total score. If the timer ever runs out, that's GAME OVER.
Happy Barm spotting!
